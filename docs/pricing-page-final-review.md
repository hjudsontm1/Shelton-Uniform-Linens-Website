# Pricing Page Final Review

This review reconciles the nine most recent pricing-page directions in sequence. When a later request explicitly removed an earlier idea, the later request controls the final page.

## Requirement Reconciliation

1. **Expand the landing-page idea beyond a single orb.** The hero keeps one focused service-fit visual, while the rest of the page now carries its own purpose-built vector systems and interactive explanations.
2. **Do not begin with the estimator.** The first screen is a pricing promise and explanation. The estimator is a separate route reached only from a secondary hero link or the final handoff.
3. **Create a premium private-client feel.** The page uses a restrained navy, cream, and muted-gold system; editorial typography; controlled depth; fine edge light; pointer-aware atmosphere; scroll progress; and low-key hover motion.
4. **Explore fair-pricing and quality commitment as the second section.** This direction was implemented temporarily, then superseded by the later removal request.
5. **Remove the section made redundant by the new direction.** The provider-comparison/market-position section shown in the review screenshot is removed.
6. **Remove the private commercial review/fair-pricing block.** That entire interim section is removed. No copy or empty styling shell remains.
7. **Replace blank areas with supporting vectors.** The hero, quote sequence, operating variables, quality, inventory models, and estimator handoff all use custom inline vectors tied to the surrounding subject.
8. **Use normal-computer margins, restrained eyebrows, and one seamless background.** Content is capped at 1180px, tested from 1280px through 1440px desktop widths, middle-section eyebrows are removed, and section bands use a continuous page wash rather than stitched backgrounds.
9. **Do not repeat the same message.** The remaining sections have separate jobs: pricing promise, review sequence, operating effort, quality value, inventory responsibility, and estimator handoff.

## Quality Gates

- No estimator form in the hero.
- No rejected comparison, commitment, or private-review section.
- Nine custom inline vectors, with no fake photography.
- Interactive factor, quality, and inventory explanations.
- Keyboard-operable tab controls with arrow, Home, and End navigation.
- Mobile navigation verified open and closed after animation.
- No horizontal overflow at 1440, 1366, 1280, 768, 430, or 390 pixels.
- No console errors, page errors, failed requests, or local HTTP errors during acceptance.
- Full-page desktop and mobile screenshots generated for long-page visual review.
- Estimator opens as a fresh separate experience.
