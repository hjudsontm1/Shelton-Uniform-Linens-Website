What moves the range

The same volume can price differently when the work around it changes.

Can simplify the range

  • Predictable recurring volume
  • Consolidated pickup and return
  • Standard folding or packaging
  • Customer-owned inventory

Adds specialized work

  • Heavy soil, spotting, or mold treatment
  • Pressing, hanging, or presentation finishing
  • Detailed sorting, labeling, or separation
  • Supplied goods or tight return windows

Your proposal should make these clear

  • Pricing unit
  • Expected volume
  • Included finishing
  • Pickup rhythm
  • Inventory model
  • Specialty charges
