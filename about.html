The Shelton Story

From West Star Laundry to San Diego commercial textile care.

The Shelton family story begins more than 120 years ago with West Star Laundry, where John W. Shelton established a standard for meticulous care, quality, and trust. The name later grew in San Diego, where the family built and operated dry-cleaning businesses across the county.

That history matters because commercial laundry is a trust business. Businesses hand over the textiles their guests, customers, staff, and clients see every day. Shelton Linen & Uniform Services exists to bring the same family accountability into a dedicated commercial laundry program.

Today, the work is led with a modern hospitality mindset: route pickup, professional finishing, responsive communication, and service plans built around the real pressures of San Diego businesses.
