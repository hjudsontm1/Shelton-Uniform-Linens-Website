John Shelton, Sr.

The family laundry story reaches back to John Shelton, Sr. and the early White Star Laundry years. More than a century later, that background still shapes how Shelton serves commercial accounts today.

Cleaner goods. Longer life. Fewer headaches.

When goods are cleaned properly, they last longer, look better, and create fewer problems for your staff. Shelton's process is built to remove heavy soil, protect customer-owned goods, and keep commercial items looking consistent over time — from chef coats and sheets to towels, uniforms, event linens, and specialty items.

01

Chef Coats That Keep Looking Sharp

Heavy-use chef coats can come back looking clean and professional even after hundreds of laundering cycles.

02

Linens That Last Longer

Sheets, towels, and table linens are cleaned and finished with the goal of preserving feel, presentation, and usable life.

03

Lower Replacement Headaches

For customer-owned goods, better cleaning can reduce premature replacement, staff complaints, and rejected items.

04

Specialty Mold Removal

Shelton can remove mold from items many laundries cannot handle, including colored event goods and specialty pieces.

Rental or customer-owned goods? We can help you choose.

Some accounts need Shelton to supply and manage inventory. Others already own their linens, towels, uniforms, or event goods. The right model depends on volume, storage, budget, replacement needs, and how your team operates.

Customer-Owned Goods

If you already own your linens, towels, uniforms, or event goods, Shelton can clean, finish, package, and route them back according to your account's needs.

  • Applies to hotels, STRs, spas, towel accounts, event linens, uniforms, and other commercial laundry accounts.
  • Keeps customer inventory separated and handled by account expectations.
  • Works well when you have established goods but need better finishing, packaging, and route support.
