Simple rules keep it Shelton.

The commercial system uses the circled S as its primary identifier. Retail and kiosk identities can coexist elsewhere, but they should not be substituted for this mark.

Protect the mark.

Keep it upright, preserve its proportions, and give it clear space. Do not redraw, stretch, rotate, bevel, shadow, or glow it.

Lead with contrast.

Use cream on navy and navy on cream whenever possible. Gold is a restrained accent version, not the default mark.

Use the right identity.

The circled S belongs to Shelton Linen & Uniform Services. Do not substitute the Shelton Cleaners wordmark or the 24/7 kiosk badge.

Prepare production files.

The S master is fully outlined. Lockup typography stays editable; outline the type before vendor handoff or package Cormorant Garamond and Inter.
